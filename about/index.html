Hi there!

We're WI+RE (Writing Instruction + Research Education), a learner-led community focused on helping everyone succeed in their writing and research projects.

The WI+RE team leverages a design toolkit to create and continually improve open online learning modules. We also experiment and reflect on our process in the WI+RE sandbox, and share the principles behind our work in our manifesto, the WI+RE way.
